About Us

Our story was born from the desire to transform the cosmetics industry with cleaner, sustainable, and skin- and environment-friendly options.

We change the way you care for your skin with conscious and natural products.

At Glowvy, we believe beauty goes beyond the surface. We develop ecological personal care solutions designed for all skin types and genders.

  • Natural origin ingredients, cruelty-free and toxin-free.
  • Formulas designed to care for both your skin and the environment.
  • Commitment to sustainability at every stage: from packaging to production.

Our goal is to empower you through a healthier, more inclusive, and responsible skincare routine. With Glowvy, you care for yourself and also care for the planet.

Frequently Asked Questions

We resolve your doubts about our ecological and sustainable products

Are the products 100% ecological and cruelty-free?

Yes, all our products are made with natural ingredients, cruelty-free, and certified ecological.

Do you have ecological makeup for different skin tones?

Of course, we offer a wide range of inclusive tones and natural formulations that fit all skin types and tones.

Do you offer facial care products for men?

Yes, we have a specialized line of ecological skincare for men, designed for their specific needs.
